About Us

Find out who we are and the idea behind Quadflow.

The Idea Behind Quadflow

The design for quadflow evolved from the inspiration of fully generated beautiful and responsive website pages. This idea further evolved to primarily generate website text content which we found was quite a big necessity for web designers and developers.

Web designers and especially website owners often lack the text or are not sure what to write and where in their multi-page website. To address this need, Quadflow was born.

Who We Are

We are a team of software and app developers. Specialized in web apps, we have provided numerous software and web app solutions for a wide array of companies and freelancers.

We design websites as well and provide tech support for a number of firms and individuals. When it comes to passion, we have a knack for minimalist design, which we incorporate in most of our web and app designs.
Finally, we enjoy writing which is what fueled the creation of Quadflow. Combined with our passion for design, the inspiration for dynamically generated web content led to this web app.
